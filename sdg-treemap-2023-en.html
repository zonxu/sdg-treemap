As a technology company guided by the mission of "Tech for Good," Tencent has spent 25 years exploring the integration of commercial and social value. Under the strategy of Sustainable Social Value Innovation, new products and projects have been incubated to address various social issues through collaborative efforts.

Grounded in national development strategy and aligned with the UN SDGs, Tencent SSV persists in doing "what is difficult but right," developing the core methodology of "social co-creation + systematic innovation + local growth" to connect digital products with the world and make technological innovation warmer.
